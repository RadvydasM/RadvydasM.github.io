Castle Puzzle Mysteries VR

A Unity-based virtual reality escape room game with hand gesture mechanics.

What is Castle Puzzle Mysteries VR about?

Castle Puzzle Mysteries VR is a virtual reality game where players attempt to escape a series of rooms filled with intricate puzzles and enemies. Players must discover clues that guide them toward solutions using combinations of hand gestures to unlock doors and interact with objects.

The game supports both VR controllers and hand-tracking, allowing players to interact naturally with the environment. Each room is procedurally generated, offering unique layouts and challenges every time.
